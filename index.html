Mental Health Awareness

Beneath: Mental Health Awareness is a beginner-friendly resource designed to help individuals recognize common mental health challenges and discover practical ways to manage stress. The site provides clear, accessible information on conditions like anxiety and depression, alongside actionable coping strategies. Whether you're seeking guidance for yourself, looking to support a loved one, or simply wanting to better understand mental well-being, Beneath offers a safe space to explore mental health without stigma. With calming visuals, structured learning, and shareable resources, the platform aims to empower visitors to take control of their mental wellness and foster a more informed, supportive community.
