Signs

Below are the four most common mental health conditions affecting people in 2025. Here are some signs and symptoms to watch out for. If you or someone you know is experiencing these, it may be time to seek help.

Anxiety

Signs: Excessive worry, fear or nervousness. Overthinking simple decisions, fear of losing control and having trouble concentrating to an extent that it is interfering with your daily life are all symptoms to take seriously

Depression

Signs: A persistent sense sadness, a loss of interest in things you once found fun, fatigue/sleep disturbances, thoughts of death or suicide and feelings of worthlessness are all symptoms to look out for.

Stress

Signs: Racing thoughts, trouble concentrating, a feeling of being overwhelmed or irritable and the increased use of alcohol, tobacco or drugs could all be signs that stress levels need to be managed

Self-Esteem

Signs: Negative self-talk, chronic feelings of guilt or shame, a fear of failure or judgment, insecurity in relationships are all signs that you could be suffering from low self-esteem
