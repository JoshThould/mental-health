Support

Anxiety

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy:CBT helps manage abxious thoughts and behviours

Mindfulness:Breathing exercises, meditation and grounding echniques can all be helpful in keeping anxious thoughts under control

Support Groups: Join online or in-person communities for shared experiences

Depression

Therapy: It's important to seek professional guidance through talk therapy or CBT

Lifestyle Adjustments:Regular exercise, healthy eating, good sleep hygiene and making sure you're connecting with nature and loved ones can all help improve mood can all do their part in reducing depression

Helpline: If you're in distress, call the helpline in the button at the top every page for immediate assistance

Stress & Burnout

Time Management & Boundaries: The Eisenhower Matric, Pomodoro Techniwue and Time Blocking are all important time management tools help reduce stress. Setting clear and firm work/life boundaries is also crucial!

Self care: Engage in hobbies, make time for dedicated relaxation period and reinforce healthy habits and routines

Workplace/Academic Support:Your companies/universities assistance programs are a great resource - use them!

Self-Esteem

Positivity Techniques: Positive affirmations and reframing, gratitude journalling and self compassion exercises are all evidence-based practices designedto improce self-esteem

Therapy:Counseling with a focus on self-worth and building confidence can work wonders

Community Support: Social connections can be a invaluable pool of encouragment
